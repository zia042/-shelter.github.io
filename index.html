Welcome to The Shelter Progetto India

“The Shelter Progetto India” is a Charitable Trust registered under Juvenile Justice (Care and protection of Children), Act, 2015. This Trust was established in 2004, in Darbaripur Village, Gurgaon District, Haryana. The Shelter Progetto is a Child Care Institute (CCI) that provides both long- and short-term services to children who have experienced abuse, neglect, violence, abandonment, and/or are in need of care and protection.

The Shelter Progetto India Charitable Trust was started by a couple, Sandra Bongiolatti from Italy and Saji from Kerala. Inspired and influenced by their previous experiences as volunteers in Mother Teresa’s, Sandra and Saji started The Shelter Progetto in 2004 where children receive care, a protective family environment and all basic amenities for their overall development.

Aims and objectives

Education

To provide children with a good education and skill training so that they are able to be self sufficient

Nutrition

To provide the children a nutritious and well balanced diet.

Home service

Children are raised in a loving homely environment under parental guidance.

Medical Care

Medical and dental requirements of all the children are looked after so kids live a healthy life.

Our Team

Our Team was inspired and influenced by their previous experiences as volunteers in Mother Teresa’s Home. Sandra and Saji worked relentlessly for homeless and poor children. This inspired them to start a home where they could provide care, protection and a homely environment for children. After Saji’s demise in 2013 and Sandra’s in 2016, The Shelter is now run by Kim Menezes who is a Trustee of The Shelter Progetto.
